New Finding

OFT: Bottleneck Inversion

Parallel MLP replaces iterative denoise. Action drops from 82% to 0.2% of total latency.

Unexpected Finding

VLA Fine-Tuning Reshapes Attention

Vanilla VLM (exp01b)
  • Gini > 0.91 — extreme sparsity
  • Pos 2 = universal attention sink (12-28x)
  • Entropy V-shape across layers
  • Token pruning looks very viable
After VLA Fine-Tuning (exp05a)
  • Gini 0.91 → 0.07 — near-uniform
  • Sink migrates Pos 2 → Pos 64
  • Entropy becomes flat
  • VLM pruning cannot transfer to VLA
Disambiguation (exp05b): 3B vanilla Gini = 0.80-0.98 (same as 7B). Collapse is caused by VLA training objective, not model size.

Opportunities

Two Acceleration Paths

Path A Compress Action DiT
  • FastVideo STA/VSA → VLA DiT
  • Step distillation (50→4→1 step)
  • DiT caching (FastVideo-style)
  • Cross-attn overhead elimination

Targets: Pi-Zero, Fast-WAM, Cosmos Policy

Path A' Kill Action Head + Compress Backbone
  • OFT eliminates action bottleneck
  • Problem becomes pure VLM inference
  • Flash-attn / quantization / distillation
  • vLLM-style optimizations directly applicable

Targets: OpenVLA-OFT, StarVLA-OFT

"Fast VLA first, serving later." — Single-request latency is the bottleneck (2-10x gap to real-time), not throughput. This is the FastVideo stage, not the vLLM stage.
