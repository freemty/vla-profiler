Takeaway: Position 2 (first visual patch) acts as a universal attention sink across all layers. The extreme text→visual sparsity (Gini >0.91) is a strong signal that 90%+ of visual tokens can be pruned without significant quality loss. Layer 21 is the most concentrated — potential optimal pruning insertion point.

Takeaway: ACT at ~3ms establishes the VLA latency lower bound. The 850x gap between ACT and Qwen2.5-VL demonstrates why flow/diffusion VLA (Pi-Zero at ~50-100ms) and autoregressive VLA (OpenVLA at ~200ms) need dedicated serving optimization. This three-tier latency landscape is the core motivation for our framework.
